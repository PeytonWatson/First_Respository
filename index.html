Major Characters

Robin of Loxley is the main protaganest in the film with after coming back from the crusade finds blinkin who tells him all of his family had passed as well as his pets. He also find that his estate was repossessed John's men.

Achoo is the son of Asneeze who was with Robin in prison and was the one who helped them all out.

Blinkin is the last family servant of Robin of Loxley who is blind.

Robin Hood, Men in Tights is a parody of the Robin Hood Story where Robin of loxley escapes prison after being captured during the crusades is Jerusalem. He comes home to find that Prince John is abusing his power while King Richered is off fighting in the crusade, and that his estate was repossessed John's men. He and his blind servant Blinken go out and regain his fathers land and oust the prince from the throne. Along the way he runs into Achoo and later recruits Little John and Will Scarlet O'Hara to help him.
